Greece


A country in Southeast Europe. Located on the southern tip of the Balkan peninsula, it shares land borders with Albania to the northwest, North Macedonia and Bulgaria to the north, and Turkey to the east. The Aegean Sea lies to the east of the mainland, the Ionian Sea to the west, and the Sea of Crete and the Mediterranean Sea to the south. Greece has the longest coastline on the Mediterranean Basin, spanning thousands of islands and nine traditional geographic regions. It has a population of over 10 million. Athens is the nation's capital and largest city, followed by Thessaloniki and Patras.

  1. Acropolis of Athens: A historic site with the iconic *Parthenon* at its center, offering stunning views of the city.
  2. Santorini: Famous for its **breathtaking sunsets** and charming white-washed buildings with blue domes.
  3. Delphi: An ancient *religious sanctuary* known for the Oracle and stunning mountain scenery.
  4. Crete's Palace of Knossos: The legendary **minotaur labyrinth** and a key archaeological site.
  5. Rhodes Old Town: A UNESCO World Heritage site filled with medieval architecture and vibrant markets.
